## Information We Collect

### 1. Authentication Information
The Extension may process **authentication tokens (JWT)** for logged-in users.

- Used only to authenticate requests to the backend service
- Passwords, PINs, or security questions are **not collected or stored**

---

### 2. Website Content (YouTube Only)
The Extension processes limited, publicly available content from YouTube video pages, including:

- YouTube video identifiers
- Video transcripts (when available)
- Video metadata such as title and channel name

This information is used exclusively to generate AI-powered responses related to the video.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

The Extension does **not** collect or process:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, age)
- Financial or payment information
- Health or medical information
- Location data (GPS, IP address, or region)
- Web browsing history
- User activity tracking (clicks, keystrokes, mouse movement)
- Personal communications (emails or messages outside the Extension)

---

## How We Use Information

Collected data is used only to:

- Authenticate users securely
- Ingest YouTube video transcripts
- Generate AI-powered answers to user questions
- Prevent duplicate processing and improve performance

Data is **not** used for advertising, analytics, tracking, or profiling.
